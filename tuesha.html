wFriends - Tuesha

Tuesha is a mysterious human being who possesses a Supernatural Magical energy known as the "Idiotic Energy". This energy is very dangerous for a normal human being as it can make them act like an Idiot for a long period of time.

How did she got control over the energy?

We all know that no normal human being can have control over the energy. So how did Tuesha got control over it? No answer to this question has been found. But according to the legend, When she joined Air force golden jubilee institute back in July 2022 she met a girl named Aashna singh. After this an incident happened, An incident which involved Tuesha dirtying the desk. She was forced to write an apology letter. Soon, she got control over the Idiotic energy.

Is Tuesha any good in studies?

Tuesha got very bad marks in every subject. Out of 60, She got below 30 in each and every subject!
